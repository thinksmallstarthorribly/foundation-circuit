## The Core Idea

Think Small Start Horribly is a specialised coaching and education enterprise with one singular focus: **the collapse of self-trust in otherwise capable people.**

This is not a motivation problem. Not a confidence problem. Not a productivity problem. It is a structural failure — avoidance of necessary action that rigidifies into a limiting identity.

The key distinction that runs through everything:
> **Confidence is conditional. Self-trust is portable.**

Every programme in the ecosystem is a different entry point into the same root problem. Each one catches a person where they are, gives them something genuinely valuable, and points them toward the deeper system.

## The Five-Stage Stuck Roadmap
*(The diagnostic framework underlying all programmes)*

1. **Competence formed** — inside a narrow container, meeting external standards. Success is visible and socially validated.
2. **Container no longer fits** — environment shifts. Individual protects the container (their only source of felt competence). Adaptation resisted.
3. **Avoidance emerges** — action negotiated, decisions delayed, energy spent on stability not progress. Internal conflict increases.
4. **Self-trust erodes** — knows the necessary action, fails to execute it. Stuck becomes normalised. Identity fractures.
5. **Behavioral paralysis** — goal mentally alive, behaviorally dead. Planning and explaining instead of acting.
